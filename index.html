About Our Solar System

Our solar system formed about 4.6 billion years ago from a giant rotating cloud of gas and dust. At its center is our Sun, a star that contains 99.8% of the solar system's mass.

Eight planets orbit the Sun, along with countless asteroids, comets, and other celestial bodies. Each planet is unique, from the scorching surface of Mercury to the frigid storms of Neptune.

Use this website to explore each planet in detail, view them in 3D, and learn fascinating facts about our cosmic neighborhood.
